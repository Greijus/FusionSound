IFusionSoundStream   GetPlayback

Retrieve advanced playback control interface.

GetPlayback (
  IFusionSoundStream   * thiz,
  IFusionSoundPlayback   ** interface
);

The returned interface provides advanced control over the playback of the stream. This includes volume, pitch and pan settings as well as manual starting, pausing or stopping of the playback.
